Elite Barbarians

Power, community and strategy

Elite Barbarians was designed as an engagement-first community platform. It offers a dark-themed web experience where members can share content, follow events and feel part of a strong identity.

Goal

Build a platform language that feels trustworthy and highly engaging at first glance.

Structure

Bring together home, announcements, profile and contact flows in a strict visual system.

Style

Dark background, strong red accents and military-inspired typography to build character.

Result

The site aims to feel more assertive, more organized and more established as a community.

Short Description

This project was shaped as a fast, visually strong platform that lets the energy of the community be felt immediately. Red accents, a dark surface and clear content blocks create a disciplined yet authoritative experience.
